Adventure of lucky Duck

There was once an ordinary duck who lives in a swamp.He has no family except for his distant grandfather.

One day he recieved a letter summoning him to visit the bank. He went and was given an envelope sent from his grandfather's address.

The envelope made him inherited 1,000,000 dolar from his grandfather.As and ordinary duck but a lucky one, he is now stuck between two options on how to use the money.

Out of all the infinite thoughts he can think of, he setteled with two choices.

Choice 1: Invests in Real Estate... Choice 2: Buy everything he allways dreams of...
